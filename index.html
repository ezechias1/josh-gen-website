Who We Are

Welcome to Joshua Generation Church

We are a movement of churches across South Africa, united by a common vision: to raise up a generation that will serve God and transform communities through the power of the gospel.

With over 50 congregations across multiple provinces, we are committed to making disciples, building community, and serving our nation.
