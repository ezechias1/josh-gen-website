Our Mission

Welcome

Welcome to Joshua Generation Church

Joshua Generation Church was birthed out of a desire to see communities across South Africa impacted by the gospel of Jesus Christ. We are not just a single church — we are a movement of congregations united by a shared vision, shared values, and a shared commitment to making disciples.

Our name comes from the biblical Joshua, who led a generation into the promises of God. We believe God is raising up a new generation — bold in faith, rooted in Scripture, and passionate about serving their communities.

Our Vision

Reaching Every Community

With over 50 congregations spread across the Western Cape, Eastern Cape, Free State, and beyond, we are growing as God opens doors. Each congregation is led by a local pastor and serves its unique community while being connected to the broader Josh Gen family.

We believe the local church is God's plan for reaching the world. Every Sunday, thousands gather across our congregations to worship, hear the Word, and be equipped to live out their faith from Monday to Saturday.
