Our Beliefs

What We Believe

The biblical foundations that guide everything we do

Core Belief

Salvation

We believe that salvation is a gift from God, received through faith in Jesus Christ. It is not earned by good works but given by grace. When a person places their trust in Jesus — believing that He died for their sins and rose again — they are saved and receive eternal life.

"For it is by grace you have been saved, through faith — and this is not from yourselves, it is the gift of God." — Ephesians 2:8

Core Belief

Water Baptism

We believe that water baptism by full immersion is an important step of obedience for every believer. It is an outward declaration of an inward transformation — a public testimony that you have died to your old life and been raised to new life in Christ.

"We were therefore buried with him through baptism into death in order that, just as Christ was raised from the dead through the glory of the Father, we too may live a new life." — Romans 6:4

Core Belief

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit as a distinct experience available to every believer. The Holy Spirit empowers us to be witnesses for Christ, gives us spiritual gifts for building up the church, and produces fruit in our lives that reflects the character of Jesus.

"But you will receive power when the Holy Spirit comes on you; and you will be my witnesses in Jerusalem, and in all Judea and Samaria, and to the ends of the earth." — Acts 1:8

Core Belief

Dying to Live

At the heart of our faith is the paradox Jesus taught: to truly live, we must die to ourselves. This means surrendering our own desires, ambitions, and plans to follow Jesus wholeheartedly. It's not about losing life — it's about finding the fullest, most abundant life possible.

"For whoever wants to save their life will lose it, but whoever loses their life for me will find it." — Matthew 16:25
