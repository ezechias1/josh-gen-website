The Gospel

The greatest story ever told — and it changes everything

The Story of the Gospel

God's Plan for You

The Bible tells one big story — and you're part of it

1

Created by God

You were created by God, in His image, with purpose and value. God made the world and everything in it, and He made you to know Him and enjoy a relationship with Him. You are not an accident — you are deeply loved.

2

Sin Happened

Humanity chose to go its own way, turning away from God. This is what the Bible calls sin. Every person has sinned — we've all fallen short of God's perfect standard. Sin broke our relationship with God and brought brokenness into the world.

3

Because of Sin

The consequence of sin is death — both physical and spiritual separation from God. We cannot fix this problem on our own. No amount of good works, religious effort, or self-improvement can bridge the gap between us and a holy God.

4

Jesus Died

But God, because of His great love, sent His Son Jesus Christ into the world. Jesus lived a perfect life and then willingly went to the cross, taking the punishment for our sin upon Himself. He died in our place so that we could be forgiven.

5

Jesus Rose Again

Three days later, Jesus rose from the dead — defeating sin, death, and the grave forever. His resurrection proves that He is who He claimed to be: the Son of God and the Saviour of the world. Because He lives, we can have new life.

6

Jesus Will Return

The Bible promises that Jesus will return one day to fully restore all things. He will bring justice, healing, and an eternal kingdom where there will be no more pain, suffering, or death. This is the hope we live for.

7

Repent & Be Baptised

God invites you to respond. Repentance means turning from your old way of living and turning to God. Baptism is an outward declaration of the new life you've received. This is how your journey with Jesus begins.

Respond to God

Ready to Begin?

If you'd like to give your life to Jesus or learn more about what it means to follow Him, we'd love to hear from you. You don't have to have it all figured out — just come as you are.

"Dear God, I know that I am a sinner. I believe that Jesus died for my sins and rose again. I turn from my sins and invite Jesus to come into my heart and life. I want to trust and follow Him as my Lord and Saviour. In Jesus' name, Amen."
