New Believers

Congratulations on your decision to follow Jesus! Here's what comes next.

Your New Journey

Welcome to the Family of God

Making the decision to follow Jesus is the most important decision you will ever make. Heaven is celebrating! You've been forgiven, made new, and adopted into God's family. Now the journey begins — and you don't have to walk it alone.

Getting Started

Your Next Steps

Here are some practical steps to help you grow in your new faith

Read the Bible

Start with the Gospel of John to learn about Jesus. Set aside time each day to read God's Word — even just a few verses. The Bible is God's way of speaking to you.

Pray Daily

Prayer is simply talking to God. Thank Him, ask for help, share what's on your heart. He hears you and He cares. Make prayer a daily habit.

Join a Church

You were not meant to do this alone. Find a Joshua Generation congregation near you and start attending regularly. Church is your spiritual family.

Get Baptised

Water baptism is your public declaration that you've given your life to Jesus. It's an important step of obedience. Speak to your local pastor about getting baptised.

Join a Community Group

Community groups are small gatherings where you can build friendships, ask questions, and grow together. Life is better in community.

Tell Someone

Share your story! Tell a friend or family member about your decision to follow Jesus. Your testimony is powerful and can encourage others on their journey.

We'd Love to Hear From You

If you've recently given your life to Jesus or you want to learn more, reach out to us. We're here to help you take your next steps.
